About Us

Building the next generation of tech talent

We started Codexa because most IT courses teach syllabus, not skill. We built something that trains for the job you'll actually be hired to do.

Our Story

Started by engineers who were tired of watching good candidates get rejected

Codexa Tech Academy began with a simple observation: talented students were getting turned away in interviews, not because they couldn't code, but because they'd never built anything real, never faced a technical panel, and never had someone explain what a hiring manager actually looks for.

So we built the training we wished existed — live projects instead of recorded theory, an AI mentor that's actually available at 11 PM, and a placement team that treats your job search like their own.
